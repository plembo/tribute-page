Snoopy, the Lost Lunar Module

Before the Eagle that first landed on the Moon, there was another called Snoopy.

They were identical, these two odd looking spacecraft with spindly legs and paper thin walls.

Snoopy, like Eagle:

  • was carefully constructed in a Bethpage, Long Island clean room.
  • had a silvery upper ascent stage, and a lower, gold foil-wrapped descent stage
  • flew in Lunar orbit with its two man crew

But they were different in two ways:

  • Snoopy never landed on the Moon
  • Snoopy is still out there, in space, somewhere
